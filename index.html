Hello!

My name is Sofia, I am a third-year design student at Western Washington University. I am a passionate learner, I love to experiment and try new things in both design and in my hobbies. I am primarily interested in typography, printed materials, and web design.
I spend a lot of my time finding new websites to explore. I am interested in the concept of a handmade web and websites as architecture. Websites are spaces we occupy, their structure dictates the ways we interact with their infrastructure and conduct ourselves within them. Their architecture should be constructed with users in mind. I hope my work can contribute to the creation of this world and proliferation of information to the public.
